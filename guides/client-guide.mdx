Welcome to Khedma Market! This guide will help you find the right freelancers, post jobs, and manage your projects effectively.

## Getting started as a client

As a client on Khedma Market, you can:

- Browse and purchase gigs from freelancers
- Post job listings to receive applications
- Manage orders and track project progress
- Communicate with freelancers through the inbox
- Review applications and hire talent

## Your company dashboard

After signing in as a company/client, your dashboard displays:

- **Daily applications**: Track job application trends over the past 7 days
- **Applications by cities**: See geographic distribution of applicants
- **Active jobs**: Monitor your current job postings
- **Ongoing orders**: Track projects in progress

<Tip>
Regularly review your dashboard to stay on top of applications and project deadlines.
</Tip>

## Two ways to hire on Khedma Market

Khedma Market offers two flexible hiring options:

### 1. Browse and purchase gigs

**Best for**: Specific, well-defined tasks with fixed pricing

- Browse gigs by category
- Compare packages and pricing
- Purchase directly from freelancer profiles
- Receive work within the specified delivery time

### 2. Post job listings

**Best for**: Long-term positions, full-time roles, or when you want to review multiple candidates

- Create detailed job descriptions
- Receive applications from qualified freelancers
- Review applicant profiles and portfolios
- Choose the best candidate for your needs

See [Posting jobs](/guides/posting-jobs) for detailed instructions.

## Finding the right freelancer

When browsing gigs:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Search by category">
    Use the category filters to narrow down to your specific need.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Review freelancer profiles">
    Check their portfolio, reviews, and completed projects.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Compare packages">
    Most freelancers offer multiple packages (Basic, Standard, Premium) with different deliverables and pricing.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Read reviews">
    See what other clients say about their work quality and communication.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Purchasing a gig

When you find a gig you like:

1. **Select a package**: Choose Basic, Standard, or Premium based on your needs
2. **Review details**: Check delivery time, revisions included, and what's delivered
3. **Complete purchase**: Proceed to checkout and make payment
4. **Provide requirements**: Share necessary files and project details with the freelancer

<Note>
Make sure to provide clear requirements and any necessary files at the start of the project. This helps freelancers deliver exactly what you need.
</Note>

## Managing your orders

Once you've purchased a gig:

- View all orders in the Orders section
- Track order status (Processing, In Progress, Completed, etc.)
- Communicate with the freelancer about requirements
- Review delivered work
- Request revisions if needed
- Approve and complete the order

See [Managing orders](/guides/managing-orders) for complete details.

## Posting jobs

For hiring needs that require reviewing multiple candidates:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a job posting">
    Navigate to Jobs → Create a new job to access the job form.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Fill in job details">
    Provide title, location, description, job type, and optional salary range.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Publish your job">
    Make your job listing live to start receiving applications.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Review applications">
    Check applicant profiles, portfolios, and responses as they come in.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Learn more in the [Posting jobs](/guides/posting-jobs) guide.

## Reviewing applications

When freelancers apply to your jobs:

- Access applications from the Jobs section
- Click on any job to view its applications
- Review each applicant's:
  - Profile and experience
  - Portfolio and past work
  - Application message
  - Location and availability

<Tip>
Respond to applications promptly. Top freelancers often apply to multiple jobs and may accept other offers if you delay.
</Tip>

## Communication best practices

### When purchasing gigs

- Provide detailed requirements upfront
- Share all necessary files and assets
- Be available to answer questions
- Respond to freelancer messages within 24 hours
- Give clear feedback on revisions

### When hiring through jobs

- Write detailed job descriptions
- Be clear about expectations and timeline
- Respond to applicants professionally
- Provide feedback to unsuccessful candidates when possible

## Payment and security

Khedma Market handles all payments securely:

- Payment is held securely until work is completed
- Freelancers receive payment after you approve the work
- Dispute resolution is available if needed
- All transactions are tracked in your dashboard

<Note>
Only communicate and make payments through the Khedma Market platform. This protects both you and the freelancer.
</Note>

## Tips for successful projects

<Tip>
**Be specific**: The more detailed your requirements, the better the results you'll receive.
</Tip>

<Tip>
**Set realistic deadlines**: Rushed work may compromise quality. Discuss timelines with freelancers.
</Tip>

<Tip>
**Provide feedback**: If requesting revisions, explain clearly what needs to be changed and why.
</Tip>

<Tip>
**Leave reviews**: Help the community by reviewing freelancers after project completion.
</Tip>

## Managing multiple projects

For clients with multiple ongoing projects:

- Use the Orders section to track all projects
- Filter orders by status to prioritize
- Set up notifications for important updates
- Keep communication organized through the inbox
