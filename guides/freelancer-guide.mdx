Welcome to Khedma Market! This guide will help you set up your freelancer profile, create gigs, and start earning on the platform.

## Getting started

As a freelancer on Khedma Market, you'll be able to:

- Create and manage gigs to showcase your services
- Receive orders from clients
- Track your earnings and performance
- Build your portfolio
- Communicate with clients through the inbox

## Your dashboard

After signing in, you'll see your personalized dashboard with:

- **Daily impressions**: Track how many people are viewing your gigs
- **Active orders**: Monitor ongoing projects
- **Earnings overview**: See your income at a glance
- **Quick actions**: Access frequently used features

<Tip>
Keep your dashboard active by regularly updating your gigs and responding to client messages promptly. This helps improve your visibility on the platform.
</Tip>

## Creating your first gig

Gigs are the services you offer to clients. To create your first gig:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to gigs">
    Go to the "Gigs" section from your dashboard sidebar.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Click create new gig">
    Click the "Add New" button in the top-right corner to start the gig creation wizard.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Complete the 5-step process">
    Follow the step-by-step wizard to set up your gig. See [Creating gigs](/guides/creating-gigs) for detailed instructions.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Publish your gig">
    Once you've completed all steps, publish your gig to make it visible to clients.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Managing your gigs

You can view and manage all your gigs from the Gigs page:

### Gig statuses

- **Active**: Your gig is live and visible to clients
- **Draft**: Your gig is saved but not yet published
- **Paused**: Your gig is temporarily hidden from clients

Use the tabs at the top of the Gigs page to filter by status.

### Editing gigs

You can edit any gig at any time by clicking on it from your gigs list. Make sure to save changes before navigating away.

<Note>
If you pause an active gig, it will no longer appear in search results. Unpause it when you're ready to accept new orders.
</Note>

## Receiving and managing orders

When a client purchases one of your gigs:

1. You'll receive a notification
2. The order appears in your Orders section
3. You can view order details including:
   - Client information
   - Gig package purchased
   - Delivery deadline
   - Payment amount

See [Managing orders](/guides/managing-orders) for complete order management instructions.

## Building your portfolio

Showcase your best work in the Portfolio section:

- Add completed projects
- Upload images and descriptions
- Highlight your skills and experience
- Link to external work samples

A strong portfolio helps build trust with potential clients.

## Best practices for success

<Tip>
**Optimize your profile**: Use a professional photo, write a compelling bio, and highlight your expertise.
</Tip>

<Tip>
**Price competitively**: Research similar gigs to understand market rates, but don't undervalue your work.
</Tip>

<Tip>
**Deliver quality work**: Meeting deadlines and exceeding expectations leads to positive reviews and repeat clients.
</Tip>

<Tip>
**Communicate clearly**: Respond to client messages within 24 hours and keep them updated on project progress.
</Tip>

<Tip>
**Use all 5 tags**: When creating gigs, use all available tags to improve discoverability in search results.
</Tip>

## Tracking your earnings

The Earnings section provides:

- Total earnings overview
- Pending payments
- Completed transactions
- Withdrawal options

Regularly check this section to monitor your income and request withdrawals.

- Join our community forums to connect with other freelancers

Remember to keep your profile and gigs updated regularly. Active freelancers with complete profiles receive more visibility and orders.
